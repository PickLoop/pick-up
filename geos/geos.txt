Building on Windows instal step
教程：http://blog.csdn.net/q_l_s/article/details/52468309 
1. 下载源码：https://trac.osgeo.org/geos/
2. 双击：autogen.bat 
3. 编译：nmake /f makefile.vc MSVC_VER=1700 BUILD_DEBUG=YES
	MSVC_VER=1400 为Visual Studio的版本号
4. 编译后的dll和lib文件在./src文件夹中
5. about need 10 minutes.

Building on Linux instal step
1. ./configure --prefix=/home/users/yourname/local/geos-3.4.2 //指定安装目录
2. make //compile
3. make install //install
